Pizza

a flat, open-faced baked pie of Italian origin

a dish made typically of flattened bread dough spread with a savory mixture usually including tomatoes and cheese and often other toppings and baked

    ingredients

  • all-purpose flour
  • dry yeast
  • warm water
  • onion
  • tomato
  • capsicum
  • different kinds of cheese along with tomato ketchup

    Steps

  1. Make a Well in the Center of the Dough
  2. Knead the Dough
  3. Cover the Dough in Plastic Wrap
  4. Check to See If the Dough Is Done
  5. Divide the Dough
  6. Shape the Dough
  7. Top the Pizza and Bake
